Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

MissingNo TCG Tracker is designed to help Pokémon Trading Card Game collectors track collection progress. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information we collect

MissingNo TCG Tracker does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

Collection data

Information you enter or generate in the app, such as card tracking progress, wishlist selections, custom lists, settings, and exported files, is intended to remain on your device unless you choose to export, share, back up, or otherwise transfer it yourself.

Third-party services

The app may use publicly available Pokémon TCG card data or image resources to display set and card information. These services may receive standard technical requests from your device, such as an IP address, when content is loaded.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not knowingly share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Children's privacy

The app is a collection tracking utility. It is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Data deletion

You can remove app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from your device. Exported files you create are controlled by you and can be deleted wherever you saved them.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
