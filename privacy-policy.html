Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

MissingNo TCG Tracker is an unofficial companion app for Pokémon Trading Card Game collectors. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information we collect

MissingNo TCG Tracker does not require an account and does not ask users to provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, address, payment details, or login credentials.

Collection data

Information you enter in the app, such as tracked cards, completed cards, wishlist items, settings, and custom lists, is stored locally on your device. This information is used only to provide the app's tracking features.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information. The app does not intentionally transmit your collection data to the developer.

Third-party services

The app may use publicly available Pokémon TCG card data or card images from third-party sources in order to display set and card information. Those services may receive standard technical information from your device when content is requested, such as IP address and device/browser request information.

Advertising and analytics

MissingNo TCG Tracker does not currently include advertising, in-app purchases, or third-party analytics.

Data deletion

You can delete locally stored app data by using any reset or delete options provided inside the app, or by uninstalling the app from your device. Android may also allow app data to be cleared from the device settings.

Children's privacy

The app is designed as a collector utility and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Unofficial app notice

MissingNo TCG Tracker is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Nintendo, Creatures Inc., GAME FREAK, or The Pokémon Company. Pokémon and Pokémon Trading Card Game are trademarks of their respective owners.
